The Story of Gerbet & Co.

“Gerbet” isn't just a name — it's where my story begins.

They say my relative, Claude Gerbet, was one of the first to create the macaron as we know it today. I grew up with that story — and maybe that's why I've always loved baking them.

When I moved to Ireland, I brought that love with me. What started in our little kitchen, with almond flour and a lot of late nights, soon became Gerbet & Co. With Liam by my side, every batch we make is a quiet tribute to the past — and a joyful gift to the present.

— Isabelle Gerbet

Gerbet & Co. began in my cozy Irish kitchen, miles away from the sunlit streets of France where I first fell in love with baking. Macarons were always my favorite — delicate, colorful, and full of joy. What started as a way to bring a little sweetness to my new home quickly turned into something bigger.

Isabelle Gerbet

It all started with her macarons. I still remember the first bite — light as air, perfectly sweet, and unlike anything I'd ever tasted. She brought her passion for French baking to our little kitchen in Ireland, and from the very beginning, I knew it was something special.

Liam Murphy

Every batch still feels personal. Every order, a little celebration. And every day, I'm reminded that love — and macarons — can take you farther than you ever imagined.

Crafted with passion, shared with love — Gerbet & Co.
